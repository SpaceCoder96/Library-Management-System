<HTML><br>Macbeth is a tragedy by William Shakespeare. It is thought to have been first performed in 1606.It dramatises the damaging physical and psychological effects of political ambition on those who seek power.Of all the plays that Shakespeare wrote during the reign of James I, Macbeth most clearly reflects hisrelationship with King James, patron of Shakespeare's acting company. It was first published in the Folioof 1623, possibly from a prompt book, and is Shakespeare's shortest tragedy.</HTML>
